Q1 2026 marked a strong quarter with revenue growth of **34% year-over-year** and significant progress across all product lines. Key highlights include:

### Cost Structure

Operating expenses totaled EUR 189,000, resulting in an operating margin of **21.3%**, up from 18.7% in Q4 2025. Key cost drivers:

1. **Personnel** (62%) -- team growth, competitive compensation adjustments
2. **Infrastructure** (18%) -- cloud hosting, CDN, monitoring tools
3. **Sales & Marketing** (12%) -- digital campaigns, conference sponsorships
4. **General & Administrative** (8%) -- office, legal, insurance
